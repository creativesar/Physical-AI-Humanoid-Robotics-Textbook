## 1. Challenges in Humanoid Simulation

Humanoid robots present unique challenges in simulation compared to simpler robotic systems. These include:
- **High Degrees of Freedom:** Complex kinematics and dynamics.
- **Balance Control:** Maintaining stability on two legs.
- **Contact with Environment:** Managing foot-ground contact and interactions.
- **Natural Motion Generation:** Creating realistic and efficient walking and manipulation.
