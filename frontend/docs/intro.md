# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics! This textbook provides a complete educational resource for researchers, students, and professionals working in the field of embodied artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## About This Textbook

This textbook covers the complete spectrum of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, from fundamental concepts to advanced implementations. The content is structured into 19 comprehensive modules that build upon each other to provide a thorough understanding of how to design, implement, and operate humanoid robotic systems.

The textbook emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical implementation, ensuring readers develop both conceptual understanding and practical skills needed to work with advanced robotic systems.

## Learning Path

The modules in this textbook are designed to be taken in sequence:

1. **Foundation Concepts**: Begin with understanding Physical AI and embodied intelligence
2. **Core Robotics**: Learn fundamental robotics concepts including kinematics, dynamics, and control
3. **Software Frameworks**: Master ROS 2 and robot simulation environments
4. **Perception Systems**: Understand sensing and perception technologies
5. **Locomotion & Control**: Implement advanced humanoid control systems
6. **Integration & Application**: Apply knowledge in comprehensive systems

Each module contains clear learning objectives, theoretical concepts, practical applications, and exercises to reinforce understanding.

## Who Should Read This?

This textbook is designed for:

- **Graduate Students**: In robotics, AI, or related fields
- **Researchers**: Working on embodied AI and humanoid robotics
- **Engineers**: Developing robotic systems in industry
- **Enthusiasts**: With technical background interested in robotics

A basic understanding of mathematics, physics, and programming is recommended to fully benefit from this textbook.

## How to Use This Resource

1. Work through modules sequentially to build foundational knowledge
2. Implement examples and exercises in simulation before real hardware
3. Use the glossary and references for deeper understanding of concepts
4. Return to relevant modules when working on projects or research

## Technology Focus

This textbook emphasizes modern tools and platforms used in the field:

- **ROS 2**: For robot software development and integration
- **Simulation Environments**: Gazebo and NVIDIA Isaac Sim
- **AI/ML Frameworks**: For perception and control systems
- **Hardware Platforms**: Including NVIDIA Jetson for embedded robotics

## Practical Applications

Throughout this textbook, you'll learn to implement:

- Robotic perception systems
- Motion planning and control algorithms
- Humanoid locomotion and balance
- Vision-language-action robotics systems
- Complete robotic system architectures

## Next Steps

Begin with Module 1 to establish your foundation in Physical AI and embodied intelligence, then progress through each module to build comprehensive expertise in humanoid robotics.

The future of robotics lies in creating systems that can safely and effectively operate in human environments. This textbook provides the knowledge and skills necessary to contribute to that future.
