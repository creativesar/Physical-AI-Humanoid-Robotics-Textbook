## 1. The Role of GPUs in Robotics and AI

Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) are central to modern robotics and AI development. Their parallel processing architecture makes them exceptionally efficient for tasks like:
- **Deep Learning Training:** Accelerating the training of neural networks.
- **Physics Simulations:** Powering high-fidelity physics engines in simulators like Isaac Sim.
- **Sensor Data Processing:** Speeding up perception pipelines (e.g., Isaac ROS).
- **Computer Vision:** Accelerating image processing and analysis.
