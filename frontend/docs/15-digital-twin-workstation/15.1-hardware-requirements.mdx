## Learning Outcomes

After completing this section, you will be able to:
- Understand the hardware components necessary for a robust digital twin workstation.
- Identify the specifications for CPU, GPU, RAM, and storage optimal for robotics simulations and AI development.
- Justify hardware choices based on the demands of Isaac Sim, ROS 2, and complex model training.
- Select appropriate peripherals for an efficient development environment.
- Understand the importance of a stable power supply and cooling solutions.

## 1. Introduction to Digital Twin Workstation Hardware

A digital twin workstation for robotics and AI development demands significant computational power. It needs to efficiently run complex physics simulations (like Isaac Sim), process large datasets, train deep learning models, and manage real-time ROS 2 applications. This section outlines the essential hardware requirements to build such a workstation.
