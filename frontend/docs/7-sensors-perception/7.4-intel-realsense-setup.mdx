## Learning Outcomes

After completing this section, you will be able to:
- Understand the capabilities of the Intel RealSense family of depth cameras.

## 1. Introduction to Intel RealSense

Intel RealSense is a popular family of depth cameras that are widely used in robotics and computer vision. They provide high-quality RGB, depth, and infrared data in a compact and affordable package.

## 3. Installing the RealSense SDK and ROS 2 Wrapper

To use a RealSense camera with ROS 2, you need to install two main components:
- **Intel RealSense SDK (librealsense):** The core library for communicating with the camera.
- **ROS 2 Wrapper for RealSense:** A ROS 2 package that wraps the SDK and publishes the camera data as ROS 2 messages.
