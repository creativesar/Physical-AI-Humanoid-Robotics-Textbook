## References

This section lists the academic papers, books, online resources, and other materials that were referenced in the creation of the "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook." This ensures proper attribution and provides readers with avenues for further exploration and deeper understanding of the topics presented.

**General Robotics & AI Textbooks:**
-   Siciliano, Bruno, Oussama Khatib, and Gideon Weiss. *Springer Handbook of Robotics*. Springer, 2016.
-   Thrun, Sebastian, Wolfram Burgard, and Dieter Fox. *Probabilistic Robotics*. MIT Press, 2005.

**ROS 2 & Navigation:**
-   ROS 2 Documentation: [docs.ros.org](https://docs.ros.org/)
-   Nav2 Documentation: [navigation.ros.org](https://navigation.ros.org/)

**Humanoid Robotics & Control:**
-   Vukobratović, Miomir, Borislav Borovac, and Daniel Surdilović. *Zero-Moment Point: The Milestone in the Scientific Career of Humanoid Robotics*. Springer, 2021.

**Simulation Environments:**
-   Gazebo Documentation: [classic.gazebosim.org](http://classic.gazebosim.org/)
-   NVIDIA Isaac Sim Documentation: [docs.omniverse.nvidia.com/app_isaacsim](https://docs.omniverse.nvidia.com/app_isaacsim/app_isaacsim/overview.html)
-   Unity Robotics Hub Documentation: [docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.robotics.ros-tcp-connector@latest/index.html](https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.robotics.ros-tcp-connector@latest/index.html)

**Vision-Language Models:**
-   Radford, Alec, et al. "Learning Transferable Visual Models From Natural Language Supervision." *International Conference on Machine Learning* (ICML), 2021. ([arXiv:2103.00020](https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.00020))

**Note to Reader:** This reference list will be expanded and refined as content for each module is fully developed and specific sources are cited.
