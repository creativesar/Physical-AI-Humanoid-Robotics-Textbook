## 1. Introduction to VLA Pipelines

A Vision-Language-Action (VLA) pipeline is a comprehensive system that enables robots to understand natural language commands, perceive their environment, plan tasks, and execute actions. It integrates various AI and robotics components to bridge the gap between human intent and robot behavior.

## 7. Challenges in VLA Pipeline Integration

Integrating all these modules into a robust and real-time VLA system presents significant challenges:
- **Error Propagation:** Errors in one module can propagate and amplify through the pipeline.
- **Real-time Performance:** Many components are computationally intensive.
- **Robustness to Novelty:** Handling unforeseen objects or situations not seen during training.
